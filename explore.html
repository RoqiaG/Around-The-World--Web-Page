Egypt


The Great Pyramid(Khofou Pyramid).

It is located in the Giza Pyramids area in Egypt.
The construction of the pyramid dates back to about 2560 BC, when it was built as a tomb for Pharaoh of the Fourth Dynasty Khufu, and its construction continued for 20 years. It's considerd as one of the seven wonders of ancient world.
You can get more information in wikipedia.

Mexico


Chichén-Itzá("at the source of the Itza people ").

Chichen Itza was one of the largest Maya cities (900-1200 CE).And is likely one of the great mythological cities (Tulan) that he refers to in later Meso-american literature. It is possible that the city had a diverse population, a factor that may have contributed to the diversity of architectural styles.
You can get more information in wikipedia.

Brazil


Christ the Redeemer (statue).

A huge statue of the art - style " Art Deco" which is made of concrete and soapstone in Rio de Janeiro , Brazil. The statue is 38 meters high, weighs 1,000 tons and is located at the top of Corcovado Mountain (710 meters).The statue overlooks the Tijuca Forest National Park, the largest in the world.
You can get more information in wikipedia.

China


The Great Wall of China

A wall that straddles the northern and northwestern border of China. Its length is 21 million 19,618 meters. The Great Wall of China is one of the most important World Heritage sites.It's an outstanding and rare ancient military defense project in human architectural history. It is a symbol of the Chinese nation.
You can get more information in wikipedia.

India


Taj Mahal

It is a tomb made of white marble.It was built by King Shah Jahan, the Mughal Emperor to house the remains of his wife and the word Taj Mahal is known for her name( Mumtaz Mahal).It's considered by many to be the finest examples of Mughal architecture , a style that combines Islamic, Persian, Turkish,Ottoman and Indian styles .
You can get more information in wikipedia.
